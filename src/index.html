HAIR GLOBALMEDIK is one of the biggest and well- known hair transplant clinics in Istanbul, Turkey

Our experienced doctors perform hair transplant surgeries for patients from all over the world. Our clinic is specialized only in hair transplant and we know everything about it. We do FUE sapphire and DHI techniques and we will advise which one is the best for you.

We are certified by the Ministry of Health of Turkey Safety of our patients – is our main priority. We provide an unique experience during your travel to Istanbul.

All patients who have undergone a hair transplant operation are granted with a guarantee certificate and a year follow up during the rehabilitation period
